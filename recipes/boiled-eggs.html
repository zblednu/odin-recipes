Boiled Eggs

It's pretty trivial, boring, and plain dish. However, it is simple, it is fast, nutritious, and, most importantly, healthy.

Note:

I eat about 4 eggs a day, a pair in the morning, and a pair in the evening.
Its up to you to decide on number of eggs, but don't eat too much - they contain alot of cholesterol(or so i've been told).

Ingridients:

  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Salt/pepper to your liking
  • Spring onion to your liking

Steps:

  1. Fill up the pot to the level when eggs are fully submerged, but don't put in the eggs yet.
  2. Boil the water.
  3. Gently put eggs in the water, use a tablespoon for this.
  4. Wait around 6 to 10 minutes, the more you wait the firmer the yolks will be.
  5. Drain the hot water and replace it with cold one.
  6. Let the eggs cool a minute or two and then peel them. Do this in the used water so the shell could come off easier.
  7. Cut them, season them with spices and spring onion, or eat it raw, you can't go wrong. Enjoy!
